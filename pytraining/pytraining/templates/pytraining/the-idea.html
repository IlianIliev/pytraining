{% trans 'The idea' %}

The idea here is to train and expand your Python knowledge by completing simple programing and/or logic tasks. Each task should not take more than 30 minutes including preparation - e.g. installing additional package or basic setup. Of course this is without claculating "thinking" time )

Tasks can have hints - e.g. "Check the itertools module." or "Have you ever head about timedelta?"

Users will be able to comment on tasks comparing their solutions with others, seeking advice etc.
